About Arc-Multi Tool

Our mission is to make powerful tools free, private, and accessible to everyone — everywhere.

⚡

We believe great tools should be free for everyone.

Arc-Multi Tool was born out of a simple frustration: too many useful online tools are locked behind paywalls, require sign-ups, upload your data to servers, or are simply hard to find.

We set out to build a single, clean, fast platform where anyone — a student, freelancer, developer, or business owner — can access 100+ high-quality tools for free, with zero compromise on privacy.

Every tool runs entirely in your browser. Your files, data, and inputs stay on your device. We don't have servers receiving your data. We don't track what you type. We just build great tools and let you use them freely.

Our Core Values

🔒 Privacy First

All processing happens in your browser. Nothing is uploaded. Nothing is tracked. Your data is yours.

🆓 Free Forever

No plans. No paywalls. No "pro" versions. Every tool is completely free with no usage limits.

⚡ Performance

We obsess over speed. No server round-trips means instant results every time you use a tool.

📱 Accessible

Every tool works on mobile, tablet, and desktop. Responsive design is non-negotiable for us.

🛠️ Always Growing

We add new tools regularly based on community requests. Have a suggestion? Let us know!

🌐 Open to All

No login. No verification. No signup. Open the tool and start using it — it's that simple.
