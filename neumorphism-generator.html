About Neumorphism Generator

Neumorphism (soft UI) creates elements that appear to extrude from or press into the background surface using dual shadows — a dark shadow on one side and a lighter shadow on the other. This tool generates the CSS for all four variants: Flat, Concave, Convex, and Pressed. Best used on light, neutral backgrounds.

Frequently Asked Questions

Neumorphism works best on neutral mid-tones: light grays, warm beiges, or pale blues. Very dark or very saturated backgrounds don't work well as the shadow offset colors become extreme.

Standard neumorphism can have low contrast, making it difficult for users with visual impairments. Always ensure text maintains at least 4.5:1 contrast ratio with its background.
