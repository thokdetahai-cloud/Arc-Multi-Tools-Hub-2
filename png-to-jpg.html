About PNG to JPG Converter

The PNG to JPG Converter converts PNG images to JPEG format, with adjustable quality and a background color picker for transparent areas (since JPG doesn't support transparency). Lower quality means smaller file size — useful for web optimization. All processing happens in your browser.

Frequently Asked Questions

JPG doesn't support transparency. Transparent areas are filled with the background color you choose (default white). Use the color picker to change it.

80–90% quality is usually ideal for photos — the file size drops significantly with minimal visible quality loss. For graphics with text, use 90–100%.
