About Black & White Converter

The Black & White Converter converts any color image to grayscale using luminance-preserving weights (0.299R + 0.587G + 0.114B) — the standard formula that matches human perception. Adjust contrast and brightness before downloading. Side-by-side preview shows original vs converted. All processing is in-browser.
