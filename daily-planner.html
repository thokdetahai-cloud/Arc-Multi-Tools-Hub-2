About Daily Planner

The Daily Planner is a browser-based day organizer with time slots, priorities (High/Medium/Low), and a notes section. Tasks auto-sort by time and persist in localStorage for the current day. Use the Print button for a clean printed version. Each day starts fresh automatically.
