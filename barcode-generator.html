About Barcode Generator

The Barcode Generator creates CODE128 barcodes — the most versatile linear barcode standard, capable of encoding all 128 ASCII characters. CODE128 is used in shipping labels, product identification, and document tracking. The barcode is generated as a scalable SVG that prints at any resolution without quality loss.
