📋 Notes Saver

About Notes Saver

The Notes Saver is a multi-note app that saves all your notes locally in your browser's localStorage. Create, edit, and delete named notes — everything auto-saves as you type. Click any note in the sidebar to switch between them. Download individual notes as .txt files anytime.

Frequently Asked Questions

There's no hard limit on number of notes. localStorage typically allows 5–10 MB of data, which is enough for thousands of text notes.

Notes are stored in your browser's localStorage. They persist between sessions but are not backed up to a cloud server. Download important notes as .txt for backup.
