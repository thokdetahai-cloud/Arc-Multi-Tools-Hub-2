About JPG to PNG Converter

The JPG to PNG Converter converts JPEG images to PNG format entirely in your browser using the Canvas API. PNG supports transparency and lossless compression, making it ideal for logos, icons, and graphics where quality must be preserved. Your image is never uploaded to any server.
