About Fake Chat Generator

The Fake Chat Generator creates realistic-looking chat message previews for demos, tutorials, presentations, or entertainment. Choose a theme (Dark, Light, or iMessage-style Blue), enter names, write conversations in the format Name: message, and get an instant visual preview. Right-click the preview to save as an image.

Frequently Asked Questions

The Blue theme resembles iMessage style, and the Dark theme resembles WhatsApp/Telegram dark mode. They're approximations, not exact replicas.

Right-click on the chat preview and choose 'Save image as...' — or take a screenshot of just that area. A proper download button requires a library like html2canvas.
