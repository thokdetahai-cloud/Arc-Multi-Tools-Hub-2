About Invoice Generator

The Invoice Generator creates professional invoices with your business info, client details, line items, automatic tax calculation, and notes. Download as an HTML file that you can open in any browser and print to PDF. Supports multiple line items with quantity and rate. No account or subscription required.

Frequently Asked Questions

Open the downloaded HTML file in Chrome or Edge, then press Ctrl+P (Cmd+P on Mac) and choose 'Save as PDF'. This creates a professional-looking PDF invoice.

The current tool uses a text-based header. To add an image logo, open the downloaded HTML file in a text editor and replace the logo div with an tag pointing to your logo file.
