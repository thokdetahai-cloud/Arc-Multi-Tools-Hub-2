About Paragraph Counter

The Paragraph Counter provides a bird's-eye view of your document structure. It counts total paragraphs (separated by blank lines), sentences, words, raw lines, and average words per paragraph. Great for checking essay structure, article length, and readability.

Frequently Asked Questions
