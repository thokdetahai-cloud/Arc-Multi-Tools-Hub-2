About Love Calculator

The Love Calculator is a fun, lighthearted tool that generates a compatibility score between two names. It's entirely for entertainment — the score is based on a hash of the combined names, so the same pair always gets the same score. Share it with friends, partners, or use it as a party icebreaker!
