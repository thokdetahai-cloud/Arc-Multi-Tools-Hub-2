About Box Shadow Generator

The Box Shadow Generator lets you create CSS box shadow effects visually by adjusting horizontal offset, vertical offset, blur radius, spread, color, opacity, and inset mode. The preview updates in real time. Click 'Copy CSS' to get the exact property string ready for your stylesheet.

Frequently Asked Questions

Blur softens the shadow edges. Spread expands or contracts the entire shadow uniformly before blurring. A negative spread shrinks the shadow.

Inset moves the shadow inside the element instead of outside, creating a pressed or recessed visual effect.
