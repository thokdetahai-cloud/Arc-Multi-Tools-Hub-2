About Duplicate Line Remover

The Duplicate Line Remover filters out repeated lines from any text — email lists, keywords, code snippets, or data exports. Choose case-insensitive mode to treat 'Hello' and 'hello' as the same, and enable whitespace trimming to ignore leading/trailing spaces when comparing.
