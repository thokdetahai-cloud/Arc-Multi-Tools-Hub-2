About Rotate Image

The Image Rotate Tool lets you rotate images by 90°, 180°, or any custom angle, and flip them horizontally or vertically. The canvas automatically resizes to fit the rotated image without cropping. All transformations happen in your browser — nothing is uploaded to a server.
