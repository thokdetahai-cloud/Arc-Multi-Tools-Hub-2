Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

At Arc-Multi Tool, your privacy is our top priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle (or rather, don't handle) your information when you use our website and tools.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

Arc-Multi Tool is a client-side, browser-based tool platform. This means:

  • We do not collect or store any files you process through our tools.
  • We do not collect or store any text, data, or inputs you enter into our tools.
  • We do not require account creation, login, or email addresses.
  • We do not use any server-side processing for tool functionality.

2. How Our Tools Work

All tool processing happens entirely within your web browser using JavaScript. When you use a tool like our Image Resizer, Word Counter, or PDF Merger, the data stays on your device at all times. Nothing is transmitted to our servers.

3. Cookies and Local Storage

We use localStorage (browser-based storage) for the following limited purposes:

  • Saving your dark/light theme preference.
  • Storing data for offline productivity tools (To-Do List, Notes Saver, Habit Tracker) which you control entirely.

We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies.

4. Analytics

We may use privacy-respecting analytics tools that do not collect personally identifiable information. These tools help us understand aggregate traffic patterns (e.g., how many visitors we have) without tracking individual users.

5. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. Please review their privacy policies separately.

6. Children's Privacy

Arc-Multi Tool does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. Our tools are designed for general audiences and do not require any personal information to function.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of Arc-Multi Tool after changes constitutes your acceptance of the new policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our Contact page.
