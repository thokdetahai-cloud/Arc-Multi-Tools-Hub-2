About Sentence Counter

The Sentence Counter provides a comprehensive text analysis: sentence count, word count, paragraph count, and average words per sentence. The average sentence length metric is especially useful for writers — readability research suggests keeping average sentence length under 20 words for clear, readable prose.

Frequently Asked Questions

Sentences are detected by punctuation: periods (.), exclamation marks (!), and question marks (?). Abbreviations like 'Dr.' or 'U.S.' may slightly affect the count.

For general audiences, 15–20 words per sentence is ideal. Academic writing may average higher. Short sentences (under 10 words) increase readability scores significantly.
