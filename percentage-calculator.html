About the Percentage Calculator

This free online percentage calculator handles all the most common percentage calculations: finding a percentage of a number, determining what percentage one number is of another, calculating percentage change between two values, and computing discount amounts.

FAQ

Use the "Percentage Change" section. Enter the original value in the "From" box and the new value in "To". A positive result means increase, negative means decrease.
