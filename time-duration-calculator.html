About Time Duration Calculator

The Time Duration Calculator finds the exact difference between two times in hours and minutes. Use the overnight checkbox when your shift or event crosses midnight (e.g., 10 PM to 6 AM). Great for shift workers, freelancers billing by the hour, and students.

Frequently Asked Questions

Enable the 'Overnight' checkbox when end time is earlier than start time due to crossing midnight. For example, 11:00 PM to 7:00 AM = 8 hours.

This tool works in hours and minutes. For second-level precision, see our Countdown Timer tool.
