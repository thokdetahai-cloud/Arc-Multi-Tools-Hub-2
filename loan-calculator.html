About Loan Calculator

The Loan Calculator uses the standard amortization formula to compute your monthly payment, total amount paid, and total interest over the life of the loan. Works for home loans, car loans, personal loans, and any fixed-rate installment debt.

Frequently Asked Questions

Amortization means paying off debt in regular installments. Early payments are mostly interest; later payments are mostly principal.

Make a larger down payment to reduce principal, choose a shorter tenure, or make extra payments toward principal when possible.
