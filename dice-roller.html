🎲 Dice Roller

About Dice Roller

The Dice Roller simulates standard tabletop dice — D4, D6, D8, D10, D12, D20, and D100. You can also create custom dice with any number of sides. Roll multiple dice at once and see individual results, total, and average. Perfect for Dungeons & Dragons, board games, math activities, or making random decisions.

Frequently Asked Questions

D20 means a 20-sided die. The 'D' stands for 'die' and the number is the number of faces. It's commonly used in tabletop RPGs like D&D.

The roller uses JavaScript's Math.random() which generates uniformly distributed random numbers — every face has an equal probability of being rolled.
