About Small Text Generator

The Small Text Generator converts your text into tiny Unicode characters that appear as superscript, subscript, or small caps. Because these are actual Unicode characters (not images), you can paste them anywhere — social media bios, chat messages, usernames, and anywhere else plain text is accepted.
