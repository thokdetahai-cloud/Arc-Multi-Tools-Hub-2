About the BMI Calculator

The Body Mass Index (BMI) is a widely-used screening tool that relates body weight to height. It's calculated as: BMI = weight(kg) ÷ height(m)².

BMI categories (WHO): Under 18.5 = Underweight | 18.5–24.9 = Normal | 25–29.9 = Overweight | 30+ = Obese.

Note: BMI is a general indicator and does not account for muscle mass, bone density, age, or sex. Always consult a healthcare professional for medical advice.

Frequently Asked Questions

BMI is a useful screening tool but has limitations. Athletes may have high BMI due to muscle, and it doesn't distinguish fat from muscle. Always combine BMI with other health metrics.

For most adults, 18.5–24.9 is considered healthy. However, "healthy" can vary by age, sex, and ethnic background.
