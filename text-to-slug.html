About Text to Slug Converter

A URL slug is the part of a URL after the domain — clean, lowercase, hyphenated, with no special characters. Slugs are critical for SEO because they help search engines understand page topics. This tool converts any title or phrase into a ready-to-use slug in real time.

Frequently Asked Questions

Good slugs are lowercase, use hyphens (not underscores), avoid stop words, and include the main keyword. Example: /best-free-online-tools instead of /Best%20Free%20Online%20Tools!

Google recommends hyphens. Underscores are not treated as word separators by search engines, so 'hello_world' is seen as one word.
