About Random Decision Maker

The Random Decision Maker helps you choose between options when you're stuck. Add your choices line by line and press 'Decide for Me!' — with the animation option, it cycles through choices before landing on the final pick, making it more fun and dramatic. Great for choosing restaurants, movies, activities, or breaking ties.
