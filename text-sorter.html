About Text Sorter

The Text Sorter organizes lists instantly. Enter one item per line and choose your sort order: alphabetical (A→Z or Z→A), by line length, numerically, or random shuffle. The 'Remove Duplicates' option keeps only unique lines — great for cleaning email lists, keywords, or datasets.
