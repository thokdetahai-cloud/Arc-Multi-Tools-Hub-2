About the Word Counter

The Arc-Multi Tool Word Counter provides real-time statistics as you type. It's ideal for writers, students, bloggers, and anyone who needs to meet a word or character limit — think essays, social media posts, SEO content, or academic papers.

The tool counts words, characters (with and without spaces), sentences, paragraphs, and even estimates your reading time based on an average reading speed of 200 words per minute. All processing happens locally in your browser — nothing is sent to any server.
