About Passphrase Generator

A passphrase is a sequence of random words used as a password. Research shows that passphrases like "Coral-Mango-Storm-River" are both more secure and more memorable than typical passwords like "P@ssw0rd!". A 4-word passphrase from a 100-word list has ~53 bits of entropy — comparable to a 10-character random password.
