About Crop Image

The Crop Image tool lets you crop any image by specifying X, Y, width, and height in pixels, with a live preview before downloading. Use aspect ratio presets (Square, 16:9, 4:3, 3:2) to center-crop to common formats. All processing is in-browser — your image stays private.
