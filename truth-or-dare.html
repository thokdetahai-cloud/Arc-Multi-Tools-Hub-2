🎪 Truth or Dare

About Truth or Dare

The Truth or Dare Generator provides random truth questions and dares across three categories — General, Fun, and Deep & Thoughtful. Perfect for parties, team building events, sleepovers, or virtual hangouts. Click 'Truth' or 'Dare' to get a random prompt for the current player.

Frequently Asked Questions

Each category has 10 truths and 10 dares (30 total truths, 30 total dares) for variety. Every click picks randomly from the full pool.

The current content is suitable for teens and adults in social settings. The Deep category contains introspective questions — use your judgment for younger players.
