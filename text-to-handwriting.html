About Text to Handwriting

The Text to Handwriting tool renders your typed text using Google's handwriting-style fonts on a lined paper background. Choose from 5 styles (Caveat, Pacifico, Dancing Script, Indie Flower, Patrick Hand), adjust size, ink color, line color, and paper color. Great for creating the look of handwritten notes, letters, or cards.
