About Remove Duplicate Words

The Remove Duplicate Words tool processes each line independently and removes words that have already appeared on that line. Useful for cleaning tag lists, keyword strings, or repeated-word typos. Case-insensitive mode treats 'Hello' and 'hello' as the same word.
