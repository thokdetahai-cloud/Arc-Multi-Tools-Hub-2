About the Age Calculator

The Arc-Multi Tool Age Calculator is a free, browser-based tool that instantly calculates your exact age from your date of birth to any reference date. There's no signup, no ads, and all computation happens right in your browser — your data never leaves your device.

Whether you're calculating age for a birthday, legal verification, school admission, or just curiosity, this tool gives you a breakdown across years, months, days, hours, and minutes. You can also set a custom reference date — perfect for calculating someone's age on a specific past or future date.

How to Use

1. Enter your date of birth using the date picker.
2. Optionally change the "Calculate Age On" date (defaults to today).
3. Click Calculate Age to see the result instantly.

Why Use This Tool?

Manual age calculation is error-prone, especially across leap years or month boundaries. This tool handles all edge cases automatically and gives you a precise breakdown — including the total number of days you've been alive.
