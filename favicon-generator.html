About Favicon Generator

The Favicon Generator creates simple favicons from text, letters, or emojis. Choose background color, text color, font size, and corner radius. Preview at 16×16, 32×32, and 64×64 sizes — the sizes actually used by browsers. Download as PNG and reference it in your HTML with <link rel='icon' href='favicon.png'>.

Frequently Asked Questions

Browsers use 16×16 for tabs, 32×32 for taskbars, and 180×180 for iOS home screen icons. Most modern browsers also accept SVG favicons. Generate all three sizes for full coverage.
