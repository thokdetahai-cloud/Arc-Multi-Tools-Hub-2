About Remove Extra Spaces

The Remove Extra Spaces tool cleans up messy text in three modes: remove all extra spaces (collapses multiple spaces into one and trims each line), trim lines (remove leading/trailing whitespace per line), and remove blank lines. Ideal for cleaning copied text from PDFs, emails, or web pages.
