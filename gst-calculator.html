About GST Calculator

The GST Calculator supports both exclusive (adding GST to a base price) and inclusive (extracting GST from a price that already includes it) modes. Supports standard Indian GST slabs: 5%, 12%, 18%, 28%, plus custom rates.

Frequently Asked Questions

Exclusive means GST is added on top of the base price. Inclusive means the given price already contains GST, and you want to extract the base amount.

GST = Base Amount × Rate ÷ 100. Total = Base + GST. To extract from inclusive: Base = Total × 100 ÷ (100 + Rate).
