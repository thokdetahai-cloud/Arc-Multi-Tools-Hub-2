About Random Picker Wheel

The Random Picker Wheel is an animated spinning wheel that randomly selects from your list of options. Add up to 12 choices, then click Spin to let the wheel decide! The animation eases out naturally for a realistic spinning effect. Great for choosing dinner options, prizes, random assignments, or any group decision.
