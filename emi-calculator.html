About EMI Calculator

The EMI Calculator computes your Equated Monthly Installment using the standard formula: EMI = P × r × (1+r)ⁿ / ((1+r)ⁿ - 1) where P = principal, r = monthly rate, n = tenure in months. It also shows total payment and total interest paid over the loan period.
