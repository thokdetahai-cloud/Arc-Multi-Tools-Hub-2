About Yes or No Decision Maker

The Yes or No Decision Maker randomly answers your questions with varied responses — from 'Definitely Yes!' to 'Not a chance!' with fun emojis and confidence levels. It's inspired by the classic Magic 8-Ball. Great for breaking decision paralysis, settling debates with friends, or just having fun.
