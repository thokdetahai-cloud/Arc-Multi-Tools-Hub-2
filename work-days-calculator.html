About Work Days Calculator

The Work Days Calculator counts the number of business days between two dates, excluding weekends. You can choose whether Saturday and/or Sunday should be excluded — useful for 6-day work weeks common in some regions. It also shows total days, weekend days, and equivalent full work weeks.
