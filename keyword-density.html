🔍 Keyword Density Checker

About Keyword Density Checker

The Keyword Density Checker analyzes your content and reports how often each significant word appears, both as a count and as a percentage of total words. For SEO, a keyword density of 1–2% is generally considered optimal — too low means the topic isn't clear to search engines, too high may trigger over-optimization penalties.
