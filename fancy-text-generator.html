About Fancy Text Generator

The Fancy Text Generator uses Unicode mathematical alphanumeric characters to produce text that looks styled — bold, italic, monospace, double-struck, and Fraktur. These aren't font changes; they're actual Unicode characters, so they copy-paste anywhere: Twitter, Instagram, WhatsApp, LinkedIn, bios, and more.
