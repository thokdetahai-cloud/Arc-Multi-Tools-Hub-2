🎯 Daily Goal Tracker

About Daily Goal Tracker

The Daily Goal Tracker lets you set specific daily goals with targets and units — like '30 minutes of exercise', '8 glasses of water', or '10 pages of reading'. Track progress with + and − buttons, watch the progress bar fill up, and goals reset automatically each day. All data saved locally in your browser.
