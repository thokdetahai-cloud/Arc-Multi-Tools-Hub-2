About Date Formatter

The Date Formatter takes any date and instantly shows it in 10 common formats — ISO 8601, US format, UK format, long form, short form, full weekday, Unix timestamp, and more. Each format has a copy button. Essential for developers, writers, and anyone working across international date conventions.

Frequently Asked Questions

ISO 8601 is the international standard for dates: YYYY-MM-DD. It's used in databases, APIs, and technical contexts because it sorts lexicographically and is unambiguous.

Unix timestamp is the number of seconds since January 1, 1970 (UTC). It's used extensively in programming, databases, and APIs.
