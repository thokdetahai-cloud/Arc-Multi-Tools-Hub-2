About SVG Shape Generator

The SVG Shape Generator creates scalable vector graphics shapes — circles, rectangles, triangles, stars, hexagons, diamonds, crosses, and arrows — with custom fill color, stroke, size, and opacity. SVG shapes scale to any size without quality loss and are perfect for icons, logos, backgrounds, and data visualizations.
