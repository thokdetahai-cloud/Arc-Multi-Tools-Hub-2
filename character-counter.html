About Character Counter

The Character Counter gives a real-time breakdown of total characters, characters without spaces, word count, and UTF-8 byte size. It also shows the most frequently used characters — useful for cipher puzzles, linguistics analysis, or optimizing character-limited fields.

Frequently Asked Questions

For ASCII text they're equal. For characters with accents, emojis, or non-Latin scripts, each character can take 2–4 bytes in UTF-8 encoding.

Many platforms limit characters: Twitter/X allows 280, meta descriptions ~160, SMS messages 160. Use this to stay within limits.
