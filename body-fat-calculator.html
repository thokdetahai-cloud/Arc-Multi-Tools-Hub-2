About Body Fat Calculator

This tool uses the US Navy Method to estimate body fat percentage from body circumference measurements. For males: waist and neck. For females: waist, hip, and neck. While not as precise as DEXA scans or hydrostatic weighing, the Navy Method is a practical, equipment-free estimate used by the US military for fitness assessments.

Frequently Asked Questions

Waist: measure at the navel (men) or narrowest point (women). Neck: just below the larynx. Hip (women): widest point around the buttocks. Stand straight and breathe normally.

For men: 14–24% is average, 6–13% is athletic. For women: 21–31% is average, 14–20% is athletic. Under 6% (men) / 14% (women) is essential fat only.
