About Calorie Calculator

The Calorie Calculator estimates your daily calorie target based on your goal — weight loss, maintenance, or muscle gain. It also provides a macro breakdown (carbohydrates, protein, and fat) using standard nutritional ratios. All calculations are based on the Mifflin-St Jeor equation and established sports nutrition guidelines.

Frequently Asked Questions

They're estimates based on population averages. Individual metabolism varies. Track your actual intake and adjust weekly based on real-world results.
