About To-Do List

The Arc-Multi Tool To-Do List is a simple, persistent task manager that saves your tasks automatically in your browser's localStorage. Add tasks, check them off, filter by status (All/Active/Done), and clear completed tasks. Your list persists between sessions — it will be there next time you visit.
