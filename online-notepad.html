About the Online Notepad

The Arc-Multi Tool Online Notepad is a simple, distraction-free writing space that automatically saves your notes to your browser's local storage. Your notes persist between sessions — they'll be there when you return, even if you close the tab.

There's no account needed, no cloud sync, and no data ever leaves your device. It's perfect for jotting down quick notes, drafting text, or keeping a temporary scratch pad.
