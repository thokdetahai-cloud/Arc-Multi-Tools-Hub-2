About the QR Code Generator

The Arc-Multi Tool QR Code Generator lets you create QR codes for any type of content — website URLs, plain text, email addresses, phone numbers, SMS messages, and even WiFi credentials. All generation happens in your browser using the QRCode.js library.

You can customize the foreground and background colors and the output size, then download the result as a high-quality PNG image — ready for print or digital use.

FAQ

Yes! The generated QR codes are yours to use for any purpose — personal or commercial — with no restrictions or watermarks.

Static QR codes (like those generated here) never expire. They encode the data directly, so they work forever as long as the destination URL/content remains valid.
