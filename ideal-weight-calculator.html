About Ideal Weight Calculator

There is no single universally accepted formula for ideal weight — this tool shows four widely-used clinical formulas (Devine, Robinson, Miller, Hamwi) and their average. These formulas, developed between 1964–1983, are still used in clinical settings for drug dosing and nutrition planning. They are reference points, not strict targets.

Frequently Asked Questions

These formulas provide a rough clinical estimate. They don't account for muscle mass, bone density, or body composition. Use them as a starting reference, not a goal.

The formulas were designed for heights above 5 feet. Results may be less reliable for heights below 152 cm.
